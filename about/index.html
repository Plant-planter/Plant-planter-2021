Land Acknowledgment

We at Plant Plans would like to acknowledge that Portland rests on the traditional village sites of the Multnomah, Kathlamet, Clackamas, Chinook, Tuatlitin Kalapuya, and Molalla Tribes amongst many other.

Our plant selector is filled with some of the most common fruits and vegetables that people grow in their garden’s, but why are these the most common fruits and vegetables that people grow? While working on this project we couldn’t help but recognize the affects of colonialism and the erasure of native plants and agricultural techniques. European settlers brought with them their own agricultural ideas and as they moved further and further west traditional agricultural techniques and plants native to these areas were replaced with those of the european colonizers. Vegetables and fruits were bred to grow in places that they otherwise wouldn’t and eventually monoculture (single species planting) became the preferred agriculture technique of the west. The idea of monoculture and the labeling of many native plants as weeds helped stigmatize indigenous practices and normalize their erasure.
